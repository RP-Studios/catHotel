Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Royal Pourceau Studios ("we", "our", "us") operates Meowtel: Cat Hotel (the "Game"), a mobile application available on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous Identifier: When you first launch the Game, a unique anonymous ID is generated via Unity Gaming Services (UGS) Authentication. We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Analytics Data: We use Unity Analytics to collect gameplay data such as session duration, in-game events, progression, and device information (device model, OS version, language, country). This data is collected in aggregate and cannot identify you personally.
  • Crash Reports: We use Unity Cloud Diagnostics to automatically collect crash logs and performance data to improve the Game's stability.
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partner, LevelPlay (ironSource), may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP address, and interaction data with ads to serve relevant advertisements. Please refer to ironSource's Privacy Policy for details.

1.2 Cloud Save Data

Your game progress (hotel state, currency balances, unlocked content) is saved to Unity Cloud Save servers, associated with your anonymous ID. This data contains no personal information.

1.3 Purchase Data

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information (credit card numbers, billing addresses). We only receive a purchase confirmation token to grant you the purchased content.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Game
  • To save and restore your game progress across devices
  • To analyze gameplay patterns and improve the Game experience
  • To diagnose crashes and technical issues
  • To serve advertisements
  • To process in-app purchases

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Gaming Services (Authentication, Analytics, Cloud Save, Economy, Cloud Diagnostics)
  • LevelPlay / ironSource (Advertising)
  • Google Play (In-App Purchases, App Distribution)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with the third-party service providers listed above, solely for the purposes described in this policy.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Retention

Your anonymous game data is retained for as long as you use the Game. If you uninstall the Game and do not reinstall it, your cloud save data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Reset your Advertising ID through your device settings to limit ad tracking
  • Request deletion of your cloud save data by contacting us
  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad settings

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Unity Gaming Services and Google Play to protect your data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
